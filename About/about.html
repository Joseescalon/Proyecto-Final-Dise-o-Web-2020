Started With a Dream


¿How we began? Started with an empty lot an lots of hard work. Our family had a little bit of money, but not enough to hire a crew of workers for months so they could build the restaurant, so we got to work. With 6 people, all family members, working day and night to build the restaurant from scratch, we were able to construct what we would name the first Blank Restaurant ever. It sure took a lot of effort to build our first place.

But that's not all.

After our first place was a huge succes, we managed to expand. The family had more money so we we're able to hire a crew to help us build the expansion but we still participated to keep the tradition of building it ourselves. With the expantion came a lot of new goals and difficulties, taxes were higher, more people were coming, and we sometimes were short-staffed.

Aside from the difficulties and challenges, we were nowhere near done, we worked a lot and grew faster and faster each month. With a lot of blessings we were able to grow to multiple locations and grow a famous food chain.


We'll keep going!
